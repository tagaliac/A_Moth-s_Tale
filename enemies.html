Lights

Moths are attracted by light.
You can't prevent yourself from flying into the alluring glow, even if it's dangerous for you!

There are 3 different types of lights to watch out for:

Yellow Light: Such a cozy glow! 💡

Street or miners lanterns are nothing but necessary for your journey. Use these to refill your light meter before you die.
A moth scared of the dark, who would have thought?

Find the next one as quickly as possible, as there is only very limited amount of time available. Additionally, use the attraction for height boosts to get to places your wing flaps are not sufficient.

Damage: None
Helpfulness: Essential to regenerate light

Red Light: Ouch, it burns! 🔥

Fires or lanterns with an open flame emit a warm, red light. But don't let yourself be deceived - if you only as much as touch the outermost edge, the moth goes up in flames!

Leave the fire to cool down and stop burning, or use it to your advantage to burn nasty spiderwebs in the way of your progression.

Damage: 8HP every X second
Helpfulness: Clearing spiderwebs

Blue Light: Beware of high voltage! ⚡

An electrical lantern is usually used by humans to get rid of little pests, among which they consider moths too. This is how all your friends died that night.

Resist the attraction to the light, as it will kill you if you get too close. Listen out for their characteristic electrical buzzing.
Unfortunately you'll still need them sometimes to catapult yourself to a new location, proceed with caution!

Damage: Kill on touch
Helpfulness: Low, but sometimes unavoidable to proceed
