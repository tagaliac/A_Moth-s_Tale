Controls

1 / 5
Moving
You move with 'a' and 'd'.
2 / 5
Jumping
You jump with 'space'. You have three wing flaps, therefore you can jump three times in midair.
Unfortunately you can't fly during rainy days.
3 / 5
Sprinting
You can sprint by holding 'shift' and moving.
4 / 5
Climbing
You can latch onto certain walls and climb them with 'w' and 's'. Most of them are covered in moss.
5 / 5
Gliding
Holding 'space' you can glide in the air.
