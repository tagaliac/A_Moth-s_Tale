Controls

1 / 5
Moving
You move this 'a' and 'd'.
2 / 5
Jumping
You jump with 'space'. You have three wings, therefore you can jump three times in midair.
Unfortunately it doesn't work at rainy days.
3 / 5
Sprinting
You can sprint by holding 'shift' and moving'.
4 / 5
Wall Walking
On certain walls you can hang and move with 'w' and 's'. Most of them have moose on it.
5 / 5
Gliding
Throw holding 'space' you can glide in the air.
