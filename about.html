About Studio 7

Funnily enough, our name came to be as a result of not wanting to chose a name,
so we just used the number of people of our group.

Let us seven introduce ourselves:

Achilleas Tagalidis

Level Design and Website

I was responsible for the level design, the website and some technicals like the save file system and the statistics. I am also passionate gamer who was really enthusiastic about creating this game (even through it wasn't really my genre).

Believe me you'll eventually become so good you will start to speedrun the whole game in under 5 min.

Alexander Ritter

Technical and Website

I implemented our central mechanics - the different lights and their attraction, as well as most of the movement. Additionally I've written and designed for the website.
I love the journey game development took us on together and all the things we learned along the way.

Like a moth to a flame - the original subtitle of our game.

Elias Altmann

Technical

As a member of the project team, I played a key role in developing the controls and user interface for the application. In addition, I was responsible for implementing animations and the logic behind many game elements, working in close collaboration with our design team to integrate the graphics into our game.

Ever tasted some good moth jam?
