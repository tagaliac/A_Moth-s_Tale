A Moth's Tale

Created by Studio 7

Introducing "Moth's Quest" - the exciting new adventure game where you take on the role of a brave moth searching for its lost siblings in a world filled with danger and uncertainty.

In this captivating game, you must navigate through a variety of challenging levels, dodging electric lights and other hazards in your quest to reunite with your family.
Along the way, you'll need to collect as many larvas of other moths as possible to ensure your own survival.

With its intuitive controls, stunning graphics, and engaging storyline, Moth's Quest is sure to captivate gamers of all ages.
Whether you're a seasoned gamer or a casual player, you'll find plenty of excitement and challenge in this thrilling new adventure.

So what are you waiting for? Join the quest and help this brave moth reunite with its family today!
Download Moth's Quest now and start your epic journey to escape the dangers and collect the larvas of other moths on the way!
