Speedruns

This page is still in an early state, as we wanted to wait for full release to do speedruns.
Will be populated during the coming weeks and months.

Rules

  • Submit your runs with the ingame timer visible at all times
  • Don't cover parts of your screen, for example with a web- or mice cam
  • Game audio must be present
  • You must at least once open the Main Menu in full game runs
    and open the Pause Menu in single level runs, so we can verify the patch number

Submission

Contact us on our Discord server to get your time on here.
Please upload the run to a video or file-sharing site beforehand.

Single level runs

Runs of each level in isolation, use the ESC menu to restart the level without cutscenes.

Single levels Any% No Major Glitches

No need to collect eggs, unintentional behaviour and bugs can be used, but no major things like going out of bounds or instantly winning.
